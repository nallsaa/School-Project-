School Achievements

Robotics Worlds
Templeton Robotics team advanced to Texas Worlds 2022

After the Cyborg Turtles won first place during state in madison, the team flew to Texas in the spring to compete against many other schools world-wide They have all put in a lot of time and effort into their robots for the meets, and did great against so many teams during worlds.

Forensics State
Emma Suneja wins 2nd place at Forensics state 2022

After participating in forensics meets in February and March of 2022, Emma has one a medal in every one of the meets she has attended this year. On March 13th, Emma wins 2nd place at state, where over 60 schools participated.

Spirit of Excellence Award
The Templeton Middle School Student Council members were presented with "Spirit of Excellence Award" banner.

This award was presented by Wisconsin Association of School Councils (WASC). It shows that Templeton has achieved excellence in leadership, sportsmanship and service to their school and communities.

What's New

  • FIRST LEGO League Robotics

    If your child is interested in applying for the Charger Robotics FLL team, or for more information, please contact the program advisor, Kathy Betters at tmsrobos@gmail.com. Applications are due on August 16, the season will start on August 30.

  • Operation Backpack

    For more information: https://www.sussexareasos.org/Operation-Backpack-2022

    The members of the Cooperating Churches of Sussex are sponsoring Operation Backpack again this year.Operation Backpack is a resource that provides backpacks and school supplies to students with financial constraints, helping to ensure they succeed in school. We want to ensure that every child in the Hamilton School District receives some of the necessary school supplies to get the school year off to a great start.
